How to save the aliens:

Your ship:
Enemy ship:

Use the keyboard arrows to move the good ship on top of the enemy ship.

Once your ship is attacking the enemy ship, use the mousepad to click the enemy ship.

The enemy Ship then teleports across the galaxy and you must capture it again.

Once you capture the ship 5 times the aliens will emerge into space and you must capture them all! Collect the aliens by taping them using the mousepad.
