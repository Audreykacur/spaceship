Use the keyboard arrows to move the good ship on top of the bad ship.

Once the GoodShip is attacking the bad ship (under the bad ship), use the mousepad to click the Bad Ship. This will give you a point. The bad Ship then teleports across the galaxy and you must capture it again.

Once you capture the ship 5 times and collect 5 points the aliens will emerge into space and you must capture them all!
